About Us

Simple, Affordable, and Spot-Free Since 1973

EZ Car Wash was opened in 1973 with the idea of a simple and affordable way to wash your own car. Soap, brush, rinse and go! All for a few minutes and a few dollars.

We remodeled and upgraded in 2014 as U Wash Car Wash to better serve clean car enthusiasts throughout Huntington Beach. Now, in honor of the original founder Jack, we've remodeled once again as Jack's Self Serve Car Wash.

Our newly remodeled bays have high walls offering privacy and protection from overspray. Inside each bay, you'll find every function you need—from soap and foam brush to spot-free rinse—at the click of a dial.

Our best feature? The spot-free rinse. We use a soft water, double reverse osmosis system built for a much larger facility. The result: the cleanest, softest water for a brilliant finish.

Pricing: Just $1 to start, and $1 per minute—cash or card. We accept quarters, dollar coins, and offer tap/slide/card readers. You're in full control: your time, your money, your way.

We also offer vacuums, vending products, sinks with wringers, and plenty of space to finish the job with a little sun and breeze. Come enjoy the beach-town car wash experience!
